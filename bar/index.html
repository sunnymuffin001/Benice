Blind Comparative Reviews

Raters judge outputs with zero knowledge of model identities, vendors, or prompt metadata. Side-by-side positioning is randomized for every evaluation to eliminate selection bias.

We discard generic crowd labor. Every vote originates from verified industry professionals on Contra who do creative design, copywriting, and software production for a living.

Subjective taste is converted into structured metadata scoring rubrics covering prompt alignment, technical rendering fidelity, aesthetic intent, and layout coherence.
