NO PRESSURES

We believe in meaningful connections over algorithm ratings.

Social networks are built to farm attention. Benice is built to respect your practice. No posting pressure, no opaque algorithms—just a clean, professional space for curators, galleries, and collectors to discover your work.

01

The Digital Card

Generate a clean, beautiful digital business card that holds your statement, CV, social links, and up to 50 high-resolution artworks. Add it directly to your Apple Wallet or Google Wallet for instant in-person sharing.

02

The Connectory

A curated, ad-free directory of artists and curators. Search by medium, focus, or region without algorithmic interference. Create meaningful dialogues and professional opportunities.

03

Support My Practice

Enable your audience to support your studio expenses or research directly through your card. A direct-to-creator patronage module with zero commission deductions.

04

Studio Visit Bookings

Make it easy for curators, buyers, and gallery representatives to schedule studio visits or virtual portfolio reviews. Define your availability and manage requests directly on your card dashboard.
